Our Mission

The public is not well informed regarding the actions of state legislatures allowing representatives to make decisions without factoring in the will of the people. Politicians are not held accountable for their decisions and need only align with one party to secure votes. Poll provides information on the processes of state legislatures tailored to the passions and concerns of each user. Any person with a smart phone can vote on bills proposed to their legislatures or issues facing the nation using our accessible app. We collect the data and show representatives how their constituents, the people who gave the representative power, want them to act. Finally, we keep the users informed on the actions of their representatives so they can be aware of if the representative acted true to their supporters.Whitepaper
